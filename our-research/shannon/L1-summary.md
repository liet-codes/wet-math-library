# Shannon's Gap

## Overview
Shannon's Gap investigates the limits of information theory within the context of Wet Math, focusing on what happens when structural transformations exceed the capacity of standard informational regimes.

## Retained Informational Regimes
The concept describes how dynamic systems (like those in Holonic Metabolism) maintain informational coherence despite undergoing continuous anabolic and catabolic changes. 

## The Gap
The "Gap" refers to the transitional spaces where standard commutative information metrics fail, requiring non-commutative or proportional frameworks to track structural inheritance and memory.
