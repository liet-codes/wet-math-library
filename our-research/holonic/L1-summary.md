# Holonic Metabolism

## Overview
Holonic Metabolism reconceptualizes mathematical structures not as static objects, but as dynamic, self-maintaining systems. It introduces metabolic concepts to describe how complex structures sustain themselves.

## Anabolic and Catabolic Cascades
The framework identifies two primary processes:
- **Anabolic Cascades**: Processes that build complexity, integrating simpler components into higher-order structures (akin to integration or composition).
- **Catabolic Cascades**: Processes that break down structures, releasing constituent parts or simplifying relations (akin to differentiation or decomposition).

## Dynamic Integrity
Instead of static equilibrium, structures achieve "dynamic integrity"—a balance between anabolic and catabolic flows. This provides a new way to analyze the resilience and transformability of mathematical models.
