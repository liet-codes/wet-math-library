# The Self-Ratio Discovery

## Overview
The Self-Ratio explores the mathematical and structural consequences of the fundamental identity $d/d = 1$, expanding it through the lens of Proportional Mathematics.

## Power Spectrum Tables
Patrick's power spectrum tables serve as commutator studies of exponentiation. They map how proportional relationships evolve across different scales, revealing structural shifts rather than mere numerical changes.

## The Crossover at $e$
A key finding is the behavior of the power spectrum at the crossover point $e$. The tables demonstrate how the structural dynamics of exponentiation invert or transform at this critical threshold, providing a proportional perspective on natural logarithms and continuous growth.
