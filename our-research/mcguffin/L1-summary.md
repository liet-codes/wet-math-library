# The McGuffin Tensor System

## Overview
The McGuffin Tensor is the primary analytical tool for Proportional Mathematics, providing a formal structure to track complex, multidimensional proportional relationships.

## Tensor Dynamics
Unlike standard tensors that track vectors in vector spaces, the McGuffin tensor tracks fill-states within nested vessels. It allows for the computation of structural changes across different D-depths and scale geometries.

## Integration
It serves as the computational bridge between Patrick's proportional axioms and Brooklyn's affine decompositions, providing a shared language for Wet Math dynamics.
